Research Interests

AI Ethics and Normative Multiagent Systems, specifically how ethical principles can be applied to reasoning capacities used to resolve multiple-user social dilemmas that occur in Sociotechnical Systems (STS) in satisfactory ways. Exploring the development of consistent methodologies that practitioners can use in the governance of STS to promote the overarching goal of fairness.
